Right to Education
The right to education is one of the most essential rights a person is priveleged to receive. 
It is the highest or one of the most highest fundamental rights a person is entitled to. Yet millions of children 
are deprived of it due to poverty, family problems or peer pressure. every person has the right to education to develop a 
well rounded quality without discrimation, exclusion or abuse. Since education is one of the most important tools a country has,
most countries imply this to boost economic performance and boost overall quality of the country.
